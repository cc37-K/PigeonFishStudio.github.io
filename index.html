Portal Not 3

The previous work from the school project. Our journey began with a spark of inspiration drawn from iconic titles that pioneered unique gameplay mechanics. The essence of our game is a tribute to the inventive portal mechanics popularised by Valve's Portal series. Simultaneously, we drew elements from the innovative adaptation found in Portal 2D on Crazy Games. Join us in celebrating a classic, re-engineered for today's platformer enthusiasts and puzzle aficionados. Dive into a unique blend of strategic gameplay and nostalgic design, standing as a testament to the timeless appeal of smart gaming. If your monitor has a 16:9 resolution, you can choose to play in full screen.

前学校作业. 作为从V社的经典Portal系列启发而来，将原先3D的Portal放置在2D的世界里，创造全新的游戏体验。推荐16:9的玩家在unity play中使用全屏游玩。

> 已支持Firefox， Chrome在线试玩：play on Unity Play now
